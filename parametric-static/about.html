About Vapor Verse

Vapor Verse is an independent blog for adult readers (21+ where applicable) who want clear, practical, and safety‑first information about vaping devices, e‑liquids, and culture.

Read the Blog Contact Us

Our Mission #

We exist to make vaping knowledge accessible, useful, and responsible. Amid fast‑moving trends and changing regulations, we focus on fundamentals: device safety, ingredient transparency, and real‑world guidance that respects readers’ autonomy and local laws.

What We Cover #

  • Guides & How‑tos: setup, maintenance, coil care, and troubleshooting.
  • Hardware Reviews: pods, mods, tanks|build quality, reliability, and usability.
  • E‑liquid Deep Dives: nicotine strengths, PG/VG ratios, and flavor profiles.
  • Travel & Etiquette: airport rules, shared spaces, and considerate use.
  • Policy Context: summaries of rules and consumer implications (non‑legal advice).

We do not publish sponsored posts or paid placements. If we ever accept a product sample for review, it will be disclosed clearly. You can browse all topics on the blog index.

Editorial Standards #

  1. Independence: Reviews are based on hands‑on use and measurable criteria. We do not sell products.
  2. Clarity: Jargon is defined; specs are explained in plain language.
  3. Safety first: Battery care, device handling, and storage guidance appear wherever relevant.
  4. Corrections: When we make mistakes, we fix them and note meaningful changes in the article.
  5. Disclosures: Any material relationship or sample is labeled at the top of a post.

Safety & Responsibility #

  • Use authentic, undamaged batteries and appropriate chargers; avoid unattended charging.
  • Prime coils and follow wattage ranges to prevent dry hits and overheating.
  • Store e‑liquids securely and out of reach of children and pets; clean spills promptly.
  • Respect shared spaces and posted rules; never vape where it’s restricted.
  • Vaping is intended for adults only. Always comply with your jurisdiction’s laws. Nothing here is medical advice.
