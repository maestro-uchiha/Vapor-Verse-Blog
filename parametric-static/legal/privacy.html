Privacy Policy

How Vapor Vape Blog collects, uses, and protects your information in the vape niche.

Scope & Who We Are #

Welcome to Vapor Vape Blog (the “Site”). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you visit the Site, read our content, subscribe to our newsletter, or interact with our pages and features. This policy applies to information collected online through this Site and our official channels linked from it.

We publish educational and lifestyle content about vaping products and culture. We do not sell vaping products directly on this Site.

Information We Collect #

We collect information in three ways: (1) you provide it to us, (2) it is collected automatically via cookies and similar technologies, and (3) it comes from third parties (e.g., ad/analytics partners).

Information you provide

  • Newsletter sign‑ups: email address and optional preferences.
  • Contact messages: name, email, and the content of your message.
  • Comments/surveys: display name, feedback, and any information you choose to share.

Information collected automatically

  • Device & usage data: IP address, browser type, operating system, referring pages, pages visited, links clicked, and approximate location derived from IP.
  • Cookies & similar technologies: identifiers that help us remember your settings and measure traffic and performance.

Information from third parties

  • Analytics and ad partners: aggregated audience and campaign metrics; interest categories used for ad personalization (where enabled).
  • Affiliate networks: anonymized click IDs and conversion reporting so we can attribute referrals.

Information we do not intentionally collect

  • Sensitive categories (e.g., health diagnoses, financial account numbers) via this Site.
  • Data from individuals under the legal age to view vaping content in their jurisdiction.

How We Use Information #

  • Operate, maintain, and improve the Site and its content.
  • Send newsletters and updates you opt into (you can unsubscribe anytime).
  • Measure readership, diagnose issues, and prevent fraud/abuse.
  • Personalize content and advertising (where permitted by your settings and local law).
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

Cookies & Tracking Technologies #

We use cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies. You can control cookies via your browser settings and by using our preferences tools where available.

Cookie categories

  • Strictly necessary: required for core functionality (e.g., page rendering, consent storage).
  • Performance/analytics: help us understand traffic and improve content.
  • Functional: remember choices like region or preferences.
  • Advertising/affiliate: deliver and measure ads; attribute referrals when you click outbound merchant links.

Some partners may set or read cookies and similar identifiers on our pages to provide their services. See Ads, Analytics & Affiliate Links for details.

Ads, Analytics & Affiliate Links #

We may display ads, use analytics platforms, and participate in affiliate programs. When you click certain outbound links to merchants, we may earn a commission if you make a purchase. This comes at no additional cost to you and helps keep the Site free.

What this means for your data

  • Analytics: we receive aggregated reports about page views, engagement, and audience segments. Partners may process your IP address and device identifiers to provide these reports.
  • Advertising: ad partners may use cookies or device signals to show ads and measure their effectiveness. Depending on your region, ads may be contextual or personalized.
  • Affiliate tracking: merchants/affiliate networks use click IDs to attribute sales. These IDs generally do not identify you directly to us.

You can adjust your preferences via our Privacy Choices page (where available) and your browser/device settings.

Age Restriction (21+) #

This Site is intended for adults of legal smoking/vaping age only (21+ in many U.S. jurisdictions). We do not knowingly collect personal information from individuals under the legal age in their location. If you believe a minor has provided us information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Your Privacy Choices & Rights #

Global options

  • Cookie preferences: manage categories in your browser and (where offered) on our Privacy Choices page.
  • Email: unsubscribe using the link in any newsletter or by contacting us.

EEA/UK (GDPR)

  • Request access, correction, deletion, or portability of your personal data.
  • Object to or restrict certain processing, including direct marketing.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

U.S. State privacy laws (e.g., CA/CPRA)

  • Request to know/access, correct, or delete personal information.
  • Opt out of “sale” or “sharing” of personal information for cross‑context behavioral advertising. Use Do Not Sell or Share.
  • Limit use of sensitive personal information (we do not intentionally collect SPI via this Site).

To exercise rights, contact us as described in Contact Us. We will verify requests as required by applicable law.

Data Security & Retention #

  • We employ reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the information we process.
  • We keep personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

International Visitors #

Our servers and partners may be located in multiple countries. By using the Site, you understand your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in jurisdictions that may have data‑protection laws different from your own.

Changes to This Policy #

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. If material changes are made, we will take reasonable steps to notify you (for example, by posting a notice on the Site). Your continued use of the Site after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.
